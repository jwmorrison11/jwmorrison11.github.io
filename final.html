Welcome to John's Programming Portfolio

For our program we made a rendition of SuperCrateBox, a popular game on steam. To play, use the arrow keys to move left, right, and jump
to collect all the boxes in the least amount of time. You can change your character from the options screen where it will also tell you how to play.
The game will let you know once all the boxes have been collected!
